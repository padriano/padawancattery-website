IP Bright Woods Yoda

  • Nickname: Yoda
  • Gender: Male
  • EMS: NFO n 09 24 / Black tabby spotted with white
  • DOB: 2013/08/20
  • Status: Neuter
  • Tests: GSD IV (by parenting), FIV, FeLV, FHV-1 and FCV negative (Feb 2015). Waiting list for HCM.
  • Pedigree: PawPeds
  • Photo album

Yoda is the ruler of our house. Most of the times he acts more like a dog than a cat, like NFO's should. When we are arriving home from work he will know and wait for us at our door waiting for his cuddles. Since he was a kitten we noticed that Yoda is a big talker and he even was able to teach that to Lothiriel! His favourite game is hunting. He is really stealth and likes to strike when we hide something from him that he is looking to. Yoda is a food lover like a proper Portuguese should be. In the morning if we don't get up after the alarm clock he will awakes us up so that he can have his raw meal.

At shows Yoda is a relaxed cat. He is so relaxed that he plays, goes to his pen, sleeps, eats, drinks and "uses" the toilet. He receives the biggest praises from the judges regarding his coat quality, boning structure and show temperament.

CH Lothiriel de Rivendell's Land

  • Nickname: Leia
  • Gender: Female
  • EMS: NFO a 03 22 / Blue tabby blotched bicolour
  • DOB: 2013/12/26
  • Status: Entire
  • Tests: GSD IV (by parenting), FIV, FeLV, FHV-1 and FCV negative (Feb 2015). PKDef n/n. Blood type A or AB (Does not carry b). Waiting list for HCM.
  • Genotype: Dilute (d/d), Non-Agouti carrier (A/a)
  • Pedigree: PawPeds
  • Photo album

Lothiriel is the kid in the house and has the nickname Leia (as Princess Leia from Star Wars). She is always ready to play and challenging us and Yoda to play. We think that she will never grow up and behave as is expected from an adult cat. Leia is a purring machine. Even when she is at the veterinary she will purr to the Vet (and also play with him). Another special talent of hers is licking. She is a liking girl going after our nose, chin, cheek...

At shows she is also relaxed like Yoda but, once again, always ready to play. She receives big appraisals from the judges saying that she is has a very well balanced body, a good head with well placed ears, good bone structure and a very promising girl.

Many thanks to Ana and Emilio from Rivendell's Land cattery to allow us to breed with this amazing girl.
