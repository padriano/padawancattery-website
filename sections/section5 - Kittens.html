Terms

All new owners will sign a contract with us to protect this kitten.
Obviously the first contact with us might be by telephone or email but we do not sell kittens in this basis. Our kittens are a treasure for us so we really want to meet you in person before the kitten leave our home.
We only sell kittens to indoor homes or secure cat proof gardens or runs.
We bring the kitten to its new home when it is possible.

We also think it is an important part in our breeding program to follow up the development of our kittens even as grown-ups and wish new owners to agree.
